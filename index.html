This calculator estimates the value of Indian Rupees over time based on historical inflation rates. Reliable data is available from 1960 onwards, and for years prior to 1960 (since 1947), the values are extrapolated based on similar trends.

The comparison with investment options shows what your money could have been worth if invested in Fixed Deposits, Gold, or the Sensex stock market index.

Inflation Source: World Data - Inflation Rates

Fixed Deposit Returns: Reserve Bank of India

Gold Returns: World Gold Council

Sensex Returns: Bombay Stock Exchange

Nifty 50 Returns: National Stock Exchange of India
